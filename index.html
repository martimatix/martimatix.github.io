About Mario Martinez

Between January and April 2015, I undertook the Web Development Immersive (WDI) course at General Assembly, Sydney. The course was intense and I came away knowing enough to be dangerous about web development. Prior to the course, I was a secondary mathematics teacher in Perth for five years. I've always loved mathematics and web development is a natural extension of that passion.


I have moved to Sydney for the vibrant tech community and also because I enjoy exploring the city. Presently, I am working at a start up called Found and really enjoy working there.
